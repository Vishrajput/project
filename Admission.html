PARAMGURUKUL CBSE SCHOOL ANNOUNCES THE ADMISSION OPENING FOR ACADEMIC YEAR 2024-25.


ADMISSION PROCESS

Document Required

Following self-attested documents to be uploaded/attached along with the application form.

For Student :

  • Copy of the Date of Birth Certificate issued by Municipal Corporation/Grampanchayat.
  • Photocopy of the Residence Proof (Any one of the following)
  • Ration card/Smart Card issued in the name of parents (Mother/Father having name of child).
  • Aadhaar Card/ Receipt of Aadhaar Application (Not Older than 30 days at time of admission.)
  • Attested Copy of the progress Report & Transfer certificate of the school last attended.(Not needed for admission to Pre School)

For Parents:


  • ID proof : Aadhaar Card/Voter-ID/Passport.
  • Residential Proof: Electricity Bill/Aadhaar Card/Ration Card.

For Admission in Class 1 the Minimum Age required 6+ years and Upper Age limit is less than 7 years.
